SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: UNGA GOALS AND OBJECTIVES REF: STATE 90254 (SBU) 

Pol/Econ Chief delivered reftel demarche September 16 to MFA Director of the Americas Department Mohamed Matari. Matari took note of U.S. goals for UNGA and confirmed that Libya's top Africa hand and upcoming President of the 64th UN General Assembly, Ali Treiki, planned to focus on UN reform, human development, anti-corruption initiatives, and climate change. (Note: Libyan Leader Muammar al-Qadhafi highlighted UN reform -- including his desire for the Security Council to add a permanent seat for Africa -- in his July 15 statement at the Non-aligned Movement conference in Sharm el Sheikh.
