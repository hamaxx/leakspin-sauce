SUBJECT: LIBYA WILL SUPPORT PRST ON NORTH KOREA 
 
REF: STATE 35897 
 
(SBU)  Ambassador delivered reftel points to Mahdi al-Majrabi, Director of International Organization Affairs at the General People's Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation (MFA-equivalent) on April 12. Al-Majrabi said the GPCFLIC was still studying the draft Presidential Statement but that he planned to recommend to FM-equivalent Musa Kusa that Libya support it at the Security Council meeting on April 13.Al-Majrabi offered that he was "impressed" that the P5 were able to find common ground and that he particularly liked the final statement of the draft language calling on Member States to facilitate a "peaceful and comprehensive solution through dialogue". Ambassador followed-up with Al-Majrabi morning of April 13 and was told instructions had been conveyed to the UN Libyan delegation to support the PRST. CRETZ
