SUBJECT: STATUS OF AGREEMENT ON SPENT NUCLEAR FUEL 
 
REF: STATE 52146 
 
(SBU) Per reftel, Ambassador on May 28 queried MFA Secretary of the Americas Department, Ahmed Fituri, on the status of the government-to-government agreement for spent nuclear fuel, stressing the importance of finalizing the agreement as soon as possible. Fituri said the agreement had moved from the MFA to the General Peoples Committee (GPC) (equivalent to the prime minister's office), for final approval. CRETZ
