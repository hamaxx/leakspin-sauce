SUBJECT: ICELAND TO SUPPORT U.S. UNIVERSAL POSTAL UNION CONGRESS 
CANDIDATES 
 
Refs: A) STATE 66814  B) REYKJAVIK 135 
 
(SBU) Ingibjorg Davidsdottir, Acting Head of International Affairs 
at the Ministry for Foreign Affairs, informed post on July 14 that 
Iceland will support U.S. candidates at the upcoming Universal 
Postal Union Congress.  Embassy Reykjavik was initially informed 
that Iceland was undecided on the candidates they would support at 
the UPU Congress (Ref B).
