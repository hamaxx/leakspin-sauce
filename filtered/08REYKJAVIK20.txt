SUBJECT:  ICELANDIC MFA PERMANENT SECRETARY ON KOSOVO 
 
REF:  A) STATE 15111 AND B) 13716 
 
Classified by Ambassador Carol van Voorst for reasons 1.4 (b) and (d). 
 
(C) Ambassador reconfirmed with MFA Permanent Secretary Gretar Mar 
Sigurdsson February 13 and 14 that Iceland will not be in the first 
wave to recognize Kosovo but will not be in the last group either. 
Sigurdsson noted that the Nordic countries have agreed to stay in 
touch with each other regarding timing, and that he expects Iceland 
to recognize Kosovo slightly before the Norwegians do.
