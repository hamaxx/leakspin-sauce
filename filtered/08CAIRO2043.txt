SUBJECT: EGYPT NAMES PROBABLE COEPSU PARTICIPANTS FOR 
SEPTEMBER COURSE 
 
REF: A. CAIRO 1788 
     B. CAIRO 1605 
     C. STATE 77703 
 
1. The Italian Embassy informed us August 26 that they 
received a dipnote from the Egyptian MFA confirming that the 
Egyptian Ministry of Interior "agrees in principle" to send 
10 officers to the COEPSU course beginning September 22, and 
listing the officers' names.  Italian Emboff told us August 
26 that the Egyptian Interior Minister still needs to sign a 
decree authorizing these officers' participation.  Italian 
emboff said he expects the Egyptian officers to be able to 
participate in the September course. 
 
2. Egyptian officers' names and titles are as follows: 
 
Lt. Col. Motaz Hussein Dawood El Tanir 
Lt. Col. Mohammed Talaat Mohammed Aly 
Lt. Col. Omar Abdel Moneim Mohammed Bolbol 
Lt. Col. Ayman Refaat Abdel Ghany Ghbashy 
Lt. Col. Hossam Hussein Mohammed El Dib 
Major Hossam Mohammed Youssef Sabry 
Major Ashraf Ahmed Abdou Hassanein 
Major Tamer Mohammed Mohammed Lotfy Shahin 
Major Wael Mohammed Helmy Sonbol 
Major Emad Eddin Ibrahim Mahmoud El Tarhouny

officers, and will inform the Department septel when the
